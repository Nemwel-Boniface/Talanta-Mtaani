"Are you ready to sing?"

Talanta Mtaani Showdown 2022

The talanta Mtaani edition #1 2022 aims to provide talented individuals with a platform to showcase their singing talents and encourage them to continue nurturing them by providing gifts to the winners of the competition.

2022.02.10(Thur) ~ 13(SUN)

@ Kenya National Theatre, Nrb.

Main Program

Register

People will register to participate. Canditates will be taken on a first come first served basis.

Auditions

Registered candidates audition before judges and only the best will be considered for finals.

Staging

Succesfull candidates will then perform to the general public who decide who wins.

Awards

Winners get awarded for their participation

Enjoyment

Guest artists are invited to entertain the people.
